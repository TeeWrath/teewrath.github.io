About me

I am a Software Development Engineer and product founder who builds tools to solve real-world inefficiencies.

I currently operate Wrath Labs, a sandbox for technical innovation where I build and iterate on experimental product ideas. My focus right now is directed toward a major, unannounced project that is currently in the early stages of development.

Previously, I founded Cooketh Company, a development studio where I shipped B2B and B2C platforms spanning immigration, employment, and writing industries. During this time, I also built and released Cooketh Flow, an open-source visual thinking application.

I strongly believe in the power of developer communities. What started as organizing campus-level events evolved into leading city-wide initiatives, including organizing official FOSS Meetups in Kolkata to champion open-source software.

When I am not building products or communities, I spend my time reading about self help, psychology, finance, and history, or exploring new music.
