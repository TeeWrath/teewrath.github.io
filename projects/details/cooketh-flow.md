## The problem

Ideas are messy. They arrive as fragments — a sketch here, a note there, a half-formed diagram on a whiteboard that gets wiped by morning. Most tools force you to pick *one* mode: either a canvas, or a doc, or a board. Cooketh Flow set out to collapse those into a single, fluid surface.

## What it is

**Cooketh Flow** is an open-source visual thinking and productivity tool. It gives individuals and teams one place to brainstorm, sketch, connect, and organize — without the context-switching tax.

- **Infinite canvas** for free-form thinking
- **Structured + unstructured** notes living side by side
- **Built for teams**, with sharing and real-time intent
- **Open source**, so the community shapes the roadmap

## How it's built

The web alpha is built with **Flutter** for a consistent, fast experience across platforms, backed by **Supabase** for auth and data. The hardest part was the canvas: getting smooth pan/zoom and node interactions that *feel* native in the browser.

> Build the tool you wish existed, then give it away.
